What it does

Everything you need after a meeting

Built on a local-first core with optional cloud AI, MinuteWave turns raw recordings into useful notes and actions.

AI Transcription

Captures mic and system audio with timestamps. Supports Whisper via OpenAI, Azure, or on-device FluidAudio for fully local transcription.

Structured Summaries

Converts long conversations into structured takeaways, action items, key decisions, and follow-ups — automatically generated after each session.

Chat with Sessions

Ask follow-up questions about any past meeting. MinuteWave retrieves relevant transcript segments and provides context-grounded answers.

Encrypted Local Storage

Recordings and transcripts are stored in SQLCipher-encrypted SQLite on your Mac. Secrets live in Keychain — never in logs or defaults.

Your AI Provider

Works with OpenAI, Azure OpenAI, or LM Studio running locally. No vendor lock-in — switch or run fully offline with on-device models.

100% Open Source

MIT licensed. Audit every line, fork it, build on it. No tracking, no analytics, no telemetry — just a Mac app that works for you.

Workflow

From meeting to notes in three steps

1

Record your meeting

MinuteWave captures your microphone and optionally system audio. Everything stays on your Mac until you decide otherwise.

2

Transcribe & summarize

After the session ends, MinuteWave transcribes audio and generates a structured summary with action items, decisions, and key points.

3

Revisit & export

Search past sessions, chat with any transcript, and export notes as Markdown or text — ready for your notes app, email, or shared docs.

Privacy

Cloud only when you choose it

Most meeting apps upload everything by default. MinuteWave takes a different approach — local-first with optional cloud when you need more power.
