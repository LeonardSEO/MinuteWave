## Short Product Description

MinuteWave is a Mac app that records meetings, transcribes audio, generates structured summaries, and helps you revisit conversations later. It is built around a local-first approach, so you decide when to stay on-device and when to use a cloud model.

## Why Local-First Matters

Most meeting note apps ask you to upload everything by default. MinuteWave takes a different approach.

With MinuteWave:

- your recordings and notes live on your Mac first
- local workflows are available without mandatory cloud lock-in
- cloud providers are optional, not the starting point
- you stay in control of where your meeting data goes

This matters for privacy, speed, and trust. Some meetings should never leave your device. Others may benefit from stronger cloud models. MinuteWave lets you choose instead of forcing one path.

## What Runs Locally vs in the Cloud

### Local-first

- recordings are stored on your Mac
- local transcription can run without a cloud provider
- session history stays under your control
- the app remains useful even if you do not want to connect external AI services

### Optional cloud features

- OpenAI, Azure, or other configured providers can be used for stronger summaries or chat
- cloud models are opt-in
- provider choice is explicit and configurable

## Why It's Better Than Typical Alternatives

Many alternatives are optimized for convenience first and data control second. MinuteWave is built the other way around.

MinuteWave gives you:

- a native macOS workflow instead of a browser-heavy SaaS experience
- local-first privacy instead of upload-first assumptions
- optional AI providers instead of one locked stack
- structured summaries and post-meeting workflows designed for actual follow-up
